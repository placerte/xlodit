# Decision Log

Records all important decisions.

---

## Entry Format

- ID
- Date
- Context
- Decision
- Rationale
- Impact

---

## Rules

- must be explicit
- no retroactive edits
- append-only

---

## Purpose

Provide traceability and prevent decision drift.
